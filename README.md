# Contacto Customer Support 
About

Headstarter mock internship project assigned by a product manager from Plivo. Full stack customer support application use to contact customer service representitive through sms, email, or live chat. After creating an account or signing into account, users can click the email or sms modal and fill out a form to contact support. They also have the option of chat featuring a chat bot and live help. The admin portal contains all the sessions for the representitive to join and help out users.

Additional Feature: Users can also locate nearest retailer by clicking the locations modal
